Message from The President

Repositioning God's people through revival for the return of our Lord Jesus Christ.

Without purpose, life is meaningless and unfulfilling. It is important for you to discover the purpose for which you were created, blessed and anointed.

What is the purpose of the knowledge, anointing, favour, grace, wisdom, power, blessings and money believers have? The purpose of all these things is to preach the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

18 The Spirit of the Lord is upon me, because he hath anointed me to preach the gospel to the poor; he hath sent me to heal the brokenhearted, to preach deliverance to the captives, and recovering of sight to the blind, to set at liberty them that are bruised,
19 To preach the acceptable year of the Lord. Luke 4:18-19

The earth has great potential but it takes human beings to release its wealth and blessings. Many parts of the world look useless until the right people show up.

Purpose is the reason for the anointing. Without purpose the anointing is either wasted or abused. God anoints people for a purpose. You are anointed to serve God and touch people’s lives. A powerful anointing produces supernatural results in others.

God blesses you for a purpose. You are blessed to be a blessing to others. God touches your life to touch other lives. Your blessing should not belittle others because they can’t benefit from the grace of God on your life.

Jesus Christ came to destroy the works of the devil. The devil is not an observer of people who are fulfilling divine purposes. He is an opponent who seeks to steal, kill and destroy.

Jesus Christ passed His spiritual mandate on to believers throughout all generations. The experience of the Spirit of the Lord upon you is the secret behind your ability to fulfill God’s purpose.

In this YEAR OF PURPOSE, may you be anointed by the Spirit of the Lord GOD to fulfill your purpose on earth!
