Welcome Message from Rev. Isaac mensah

We are grateful unto God for seeing us through the year and another season is here with us again. I believe it has been by His mercies. I want to use this occasion to remind ourselves of the fact that God has made us stewards. The resources and people God caused to come our way are things we should not take for granted because as stewards a day for accountability will come. Continue to visit our website, keep listening to Fountain Gate Chapel programs both on TV, Radio and on the internet.
